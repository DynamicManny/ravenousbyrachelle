Good to Know

Before you order

Island-Wide Delivery

Delivery is included across Barbados. We are based in St. George central to the island which means reliable, timely delivery to every parish.

Lead Times

Standard orders require 72 hours notice. Custom and celebration cakes require a minimum of 14 days to allow for consultation, sourcing, and preparation. Wedding cakes require a minimum of 30 days.

Deposit Policy

A 50% deposit is required to confirm all orders. This is a standard practice for bespoke bakery work and guarantees your slot and your ingredients. The balance is due on delivery.
